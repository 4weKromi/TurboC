

## Things to remember when writing draft(txt)

### Syntax:

*Keys between c1 to c7 (6 octaves)

*Keys are separate with '*' and '#'

*Delay are separate with '@' and '&'

*Delay are in milliseconds

*New line and spaces are only allowed between '&' and '*'


### example:
*c4@350& C scale*d4@350&
you can add information between keys*e4@350&
*f4@350&text here are ignored*g4@350&*a4@350&*b4@350&*c3@350&
